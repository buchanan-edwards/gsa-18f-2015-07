Getting Started

My Medicine Cabinet was created to help keep you informed about current medication recalls and adverse events. It is also simple to use.

  1. Create Profile

    Either fill the form out on the Home Page or visit Create Profile.

  2. Add Medications

    From My Medications: Type the name of your medication in the search box.

    Select it from the list below, and choose Add Medication.

  3. View Details

    Under Current Medications, you will see a list of medications you have added.

    Click the View Details button.

  4. Manage

    Keep your list up to date.

    To remove a medication from your list, click the View Details and the Remove button.
